BIKER

BIKER (“Bayesian Inference of the K600 Evasion Rate”) enables inference of the normalized riverine gas exchange velocity from measurements of river surface width and height/slope. It does so using Bayesian inference and a Hamiltonian Monte Carlo sampler to generate a posterior distribution for a gas exchange model. BIKER was developed in the context of the upcoming NASA/CNES SWOT mission.
